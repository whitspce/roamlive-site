Live stream from your phone.
Free + open source.

Download for Android

Features

  • Stealth mode

    One tap and the screen goes black but the stream keeps going. Press and hold anywhere to bring it back. Useful at concerts where waving a bright phone around is obnoxious, or in areas where you don’t want to draw attention to filming gear.

  • BRB screen

    One tap BRB screen with custom text built in. Mutes camera and mic with one press.

  • Predictive thermal management

    The phone tells you before it overheats and drops your bitrate to keep the stream alive. Surfaces what it’s doing instead of silently throttling.

  • Chat panel, for you only

    Read Twitch and Kick chat side by side without burning it into your broadcast.

Upcoming features

  • Torch / flashlight toggle for night streams
  • Auto-reconnect when the network blips
  • External mic picker (USB-C, DJI Mic, etc.)
  • Multi-destination streaming (Twitch + Kick + YouTube at once)

Private by design

  • Google Play services not required to run
  • No telemetry, no analytics, no tracking
  • Talks only to the streaming server you point it at
  • Open source on GitHub under GPL‑3.0

How to install

  1. Tap the download button above. You’ll get an .apk file.
  2. Open it. Android will warn you it’s not from the Play Store (by design). When it asks, allow your file manager (or whatever app you opened it from) to install unknown apps.
  3. That’s it. Open Roam Live, grant camera + mic, paste your stream URL and key in Settings, hold to go live.
